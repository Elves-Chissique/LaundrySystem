Ho the dry - cleaning was discovered?
The discovery of dry cleaning is often attributed to a fortuitous accident by a French dye-works owner named Jean-Baptiste Jolly. In the mid-19th century, Jolly noticed that a kerosene spill on a fabric not only removed the stain but also left the cloth cleaner and brighter. Recognizing the potential of this accidental discovery, he experimented further and refined the process.
